# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project aims for a respectful, calm, and constructive collaboration style.

Examples of expected behavior:

- be respectful and professional
- discuss ideas and behavior, not people
- give constructive feedback
- assume good intent where possible
- help keep discussions understandable and focused

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- personal attacks
- harassment
- insulting or aggressive language
- repeated disruptive behavior
- publishing private information of others without permission

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, documentation feedback, and any
other public interaction connected to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or
discussions that do not fit this standard.

## Contact

If conduct becomes a problem, please contact the maintainers privately instead
of escalating the issue publicly.
